What is Happening in Yemen?

Yemen is at war with Saudi Arabia, which is leaving millions of people hungry, and in need of medical help and shelter.

All of this information is from a BBC News article.

24 million people (80% of the population) need protection and humanitarian assistance. Around 20 million people need help securing food. 10 million of them are close to famine.
Around 2 million children are acutely malnourished.
20 million people lack reasonable healthcare
18 million people do not have access to clean water or sanitation.
3.65 million people have been displaced from their home

This has been the second most lethal war in the world.

Yemen needs our help.
