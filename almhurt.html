ALM: Why is it hurtful?

ALM stands for "all lives matter." The use of this term started as a comeback to the phrase "black lives matter." Here is why it's racist.

Of course; all lives do matter. That's the goal. White lives have always mattered, but black lives haven't been treated like they mattered. Saying "all lives matter" after someone says "black lives matter" takes away the power that statement has. It silences the movement.

Blue Lives Matter

"Blue lives matter" refers to cops and their lives. However, "blue lives" are only "blue lives" for a certain amount of hours each week. Black folks live in their skin 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. They fear discrimination and prejudice every second, yet "Blue lives" can take off the uniform.
