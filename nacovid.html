COVID-19 is Disproportionately Affecting Native Americans

Not only health wise, but economy wise too.

Tribal businesses are closing. Tribal governments don't have the funds to run clinics. Tribal governments should receive $8 billion under the Coronavirus, Aid, Relief, and CARES Act, to respond to this crisis, but they need our help.

Tribal governments also have a lack of access to clean water and housing challenges due to COVID-19.
