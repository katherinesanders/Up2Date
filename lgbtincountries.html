This list looks long, but it is only 29 countries that have legalized gay marriage so far. The list of countries that have NOT legalized gay marriage would be aroung 6.3 times longer than this. Even though these countries have made gay marriage legal, that does not mean members of the LGBTQ+ community are treated fairly.

It is completely illegal to be LGBTQ+ in 71 countries. That is double the length of this list. Even in Egypt, where being gay is not illegal, Sara Hegazy, an LGBTQ activist, was tortured for raising the pride flag at a protest. She was charged with "promoting sexual deviancy and debauchery," and spent three months in prison before being released on bail. After the torture she endured, PTSD drove her to taking her life.

Say her name: Sara Hegazy.
