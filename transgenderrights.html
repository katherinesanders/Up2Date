Transgender Rights

How are transgender folks treated differently in the US?

Transgender and Intersex individuals are not getting the education they need in schools.

Only 18 US states have hate crime laws regarding gender identity. The states that do not are:

Alabama, Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Florida, Georiga, Idaho, Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Kentucky, Louisiana, Maine, Maryland, Michigan, Minnesota, Mississippi, Missouri, Montana, Nebraska, Nevada, North Carolina, North Dakota, Ohio, Oklahoma, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, South Dakota, Texas, Utah, and West Virginia.

In 2018, 189 hate crimes against people based on their gender identity. Keep in mind, only 18 states consider gender identity a hate crime, and not all are reported. This number could be much MUCH higher.
