Missing and Murdered Indigenous Women

NO MORE STOLEN SISTERS, MOTHERS, COUSINS, AND AUNTIES.

5,712 Indigenous women were recorded missing in 2016.

84% of Native Women have experienced violence in their lifetime.

Montana, Alaska, and New Mexico have reported the highest cases of MMIW, yet they all have populations lower than 2.1 million. These are the only reported cases. The real number of MMIW is much higher than what we know now.
